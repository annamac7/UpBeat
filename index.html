Getting Started

Familiarize yourself with loveSong.

Link Your Spotify

Let us know your music taste

With access to your Spotify account, loveSong will create a profile that showcases your top playlists, artists, and tracks. Forget coming up with the perfect dating app profile, your music speaks for itself.


Your music taste is unique to you. Maybe you're a country girl or a Drake superfan, either way connect you with people who share your passions.

Find Your Musical Match

New People. Same Music

Now that your profile is ready, it's time to meet people! loveSong's musically in-touch algorithm will connect you with people who share the same musical taste as you.


While it maybe difficult to find someone as into Swedish house music as you are, loveSong will provide matches that still share those same core listening habits.

Stay Connected

Message your matches

When you match with someone, we'll connect you via the social media of your choosing. We know it can be scary starting conversations with new people, that's why with each match loveSong gives you a list of your shared favorite artists and tracks. That's a heck of an intro.


What are you waiting for? Get ready to meet your musical match!
